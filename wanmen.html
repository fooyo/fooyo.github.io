Fooyo Case | Wanmen University 万门大学
Wanmen University(万门大学)
A collaborative video and quiz system with integration made across website and mobile devices.

Online education has become viral. Trumpeted by visionary educators and aspirant entrepreneurs, great products emerged constantly. For example, Coursera, Udacity, Udemy, KhanAcademy and Duolingo are the pioneers in this industry. However, when China thought but it, there was language and cultural gap to bridge. Fooyo took up the challenge and made it come true! With the mission to bring education accessible to Chinese speakers, we collaborated with Wanmen University and brought a learning Utopia alive.
