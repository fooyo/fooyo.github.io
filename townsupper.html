Town Supper
An intelligent app that soothes hungry souls by providing supper to on-campus students

Food only tastes better when you can get them whenever you have cravings. Town Supper app was made simple, clear and easy-to-use for students in National University of Singapore to order supper. Collaborating with Town Supper, Fooyo developed an app that has simplified ordering and stocktaking for both the consumers and the storeowners. We built an app that is productive, self-sustaining and well-loved.
